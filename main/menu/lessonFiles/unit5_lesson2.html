Short Summary
Trigonometry identities are one of the most significant pieces of information to memorize, especially the first couple of identities. So, what exactly are them? Well, to begin with, trigonometry identities are expressions containing trigonometry that will always be true. This is extremely useful for simplifying expressions and even being able to solve some them. The three most significant ones that you should memorize are: $$\color{#04afff}{\sin^2\theta + \cos^2\theta=1\\\\1+\tan^2\theta=\sec^2\theta\\\\1+\cot^2\theta=\csc^2\theta}$$ While some other useful trigonometric relationships to memorize are: $$\color{#04afff}{\tan\theta=\frac{\sin\theta}{\cos\theta}\\\\\cot\theta=\frac{\cos\theta}{\sin\theta}\\\\\csc\theta=\frac{1}{\sin\theta}\\\\\sec\theta=\frac{1}{\cos\theta}}$$
