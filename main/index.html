About Us
We, the developers, are firm believers of the fact that prerequisite mathematical subjects, such as precalculus, are essential for students to succeed in higher levels of math. Many significant, high paying jobs require higher levels of math, especially those pertaining to computer science, physics, engineering, and more. To encourage the learning process, we have decided to make this website not only interactive and convenient, but also capable of providing feedback on what the user should concentrate on.
